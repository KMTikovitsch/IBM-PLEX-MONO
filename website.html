About the Font

IBM Plex is a font styled and designed by Mike Abbink, IBM BX&D, and collabed with Bold Monday. This font was meant to capture the history of IBM’s typefaces. This font was designed for legibility in print and for screens. The font includes Sans, Condensed Sans, Mono, Serif, and other different styles. However, the style focused on here is the “Mono” styled font.

The Designers...

Mike Abbink is the creative director at IBM Brant Experience & Design. They oversee a team working on expression and evolving IBM. They have an experienced career as a type designer, creating many type families, some of which are: FF Kievit, FF Milo, and Brando.

Bold Monday is Dutch type foundry established in 2008. They encompass a library of custom typeface design for international clients, as well as creating retail fonts for designers.
